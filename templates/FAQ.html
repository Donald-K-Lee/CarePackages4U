Frequently Asked Questions:

Which locations does your app work?

-- Our site is intended for use throughout Canada. However, we are always looking for ways to expand

How do I get my items?

-- Donors will first contact you through your email or by phone and arrange a delivery date with you.

What if no one accepts my request?

-- If no one accepts your request, you can simply create a new listing.

How can I protect myself from scams?

-- Beware: Do not accept any aid from anyone demanding for payment! This is most likely a scam. Also check out the Government of Canada's website to find out more about recent scams.

How long until I can expect my delivery?

-- You can expect to recieve your goods within 10 days.
